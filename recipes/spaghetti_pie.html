Spaghetti Pie

Spaghetti pie is a hearty and delicious dish that combines spaghetti with a savory meat sauce and cheese, baked into a pie shape.

Ingredients:

  • 8 oz spaghetti
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (14.5 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 can (8 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/4 tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Cooking spray

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
  2. Cook the spaghetti according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, brown the ground beef over medium-high heat, breaking it up with a spatula. Add the onion, bell pepper, and garlic, and cook for 5-7 minutes, or until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Add the diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes (if using) to the skillet. Stir to combine and bring the mixture to a simmer. Let it cook for 10-15 minutes, or until the sauce has thickened.
  5. In a large mixing bowl, whisk the eggs, Parmesan cheese, and ricotta cheese together.
  6. Add the cooked spaghetti to the bowl and toss to coat the noodles with the cheese mixture.
  7. Grease a 9-inch pie dish with cooking spray. Arrange half of the spaghetti mixture in the bottom of the dish.
  8. Spoon half of the meat sauce over the spaghetti layer.
  9. Sprinkle 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese over the meat sauce.
  10. Repeat the layers with the remaining spaghetti mixture, meat sauce, and mozzarella cheese.
  11. Cover the dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and golden brown.
  13. Let the spaghetti pie cool for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
